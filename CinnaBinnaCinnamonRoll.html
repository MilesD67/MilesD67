Cinna Binna Cinnamon Roll

Written by: George Mcbinna Mcinnamon toast crunch

Published: December 8 2067

Do you want to make a Cinnamon Roll but dont have the ingredients? Follow these instruction to make the most scrumptious rolls!

Instructions:
  • Cinnamon
  • 2mg Baking Powder
  • 50g Sugar
  • Perfect White Duck
  • White Flower(not flour)
  • Water? Idk don't we always need water?
  • Ummm salt, prob like 200g?
  • Oh! An egg, I almost forgot, go to Africa and grab an ostrich egg!
  • BUTTER! That should be it, I think?
Preheat the oven to a preheated tempreture of 1000 degrees. Mix the baking powder with the cinnamon and white flower until the mix is mixed. Then put in the water, ostrich egg, and butter and heat it so its heated at 300 degrees. Then add the 200 grams of salt and 50 grams of diabetic sugar in to the mixed mix. Next put in the white duck to tenderize the mixture and leave out for 30 days to oxidize. Dispose of the substance and then make your white duck go to the store and buy cinnamon rolls and come back, and you just made cinnamon rolls!
